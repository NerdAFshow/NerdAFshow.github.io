Welcome to the NerdAF Homepage!



We are the NerdAF podcast and we are all about open conversations on how
nerd culture is impacting the world around us, and examining big issues through their presentation
in pop culture.

We do this by bringing together hosts and guests of various viewpoints to share
and discuss topics that dive deeper into the changing landscape of
pop culture and the impact nerd culture is having in that landscape.
